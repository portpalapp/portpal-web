Dispatch System

How longshoremen get dispatched — the circular button system.

The Button — How Dispatch Works

1. Call In (Night Before)

Workers call in by 10 PM to declare they're available for tomorrow's dispatch. The DOA system tracks how many declared per category.

2. Plug In (Morning Of)

Workers physically swipe their card at the dispatch hall. If you don't plug in, you're invisible to all buttons — completely skipped.

3. Button Walk

Each job category has a rotating button (circular token). It walks forward from where it stopped last time, grabbing rated + plugged-in workers. Wraps around at end of board.

General work — catch-all for workers not grabbed by specialty buttons

Each category fills ALL jobs across ALL boards (A→B→C→T→00→R) before the next category starts. Workers claimed by earlier buttons are removed from later ones.
