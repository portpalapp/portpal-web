Cohort Retention Heatmap (% active in month N)
How to read: Each row is a signup cohort. Column N shows the % of that cohort still active N months after joining. The Dec 2024 cohort (129 users, your biggest early wave) shows exceptional retention: 69% at month 12. Jan 2025 (198 users) stabilized around 49-52% long-term.
First Week Shifts → Long-term Retention
Magic number: Users who log 6+ shifts in their first week retain at dramatically higher rates. This is your activation threshold — focus onboarding around getting users to log their first week of shifts.
