- Over 2,500 shifts tracked
- Most popular job: Tractor Driver at Centennial

## How We Use Your Data
Your data privacy is crucial. Here's what you should know:
- All data is encrypted and stored securely
- We never share individual data with anyone
- Statistics are only shown in aggregate
- You own your data and can delete it anytime
- I observe usage patterns to improve features and calculations
