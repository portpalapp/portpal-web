## 4. CONTRACT NEGOTIATION PERIODS

### Background: ILWU Contract Cycles

**ILWU Canada / BCMEA Master Agreement:**
- Current contract: Typically 3-4 year terms
- Last major negotiation: 2023 (resulted in July 2023 strike)
- Next anticipated negotiation: 2027 (3-year deal) or when contract expires
- Rate changes: Typically April 1 annually (within contract)

**Key Dates to Track:**
- Contract expiration date (monitor ILWU Local 500/502 announcements)
- Strike authorization votes
- Ratification votes
- New rate effective dates (usually April 1)

### Campaign: "Rate Change Ready"

**Timing:** 2 weeks before + 2 weeks after rate changes (typically April)

| Content | Platform | Message |
