## 7. Brand Voice Guide

### Core Voice Attributes

**Confident but not arrogant**
- We know our product solves a real problem
- We don't talk down to our audience
- We respect the expertise they already have

**Helpful, not preachy**
- We inform without lecturing
- We offer solutions without judgment
- We acknowledge the complexity they deal with

**Relatable, not trying too hard**
- We understand the work
- We speak their language
- We don't force humor

**Professional with personality**
- We can be casual without being unprofessional
- We can be serious when it matters
- We match the tone of the platform

---

### Do's

1. **DO speak to the complexity** - Acknowledge that pay calculation IS complicated. Don't oversimplify their experience.

2. **DO respect their intelligence** - They know their jobs. We just make one part easier.

3. **DO use specific examples** - Concrete numbers and scenarios build trust.

4. **DO acknowledge the system, not blame individuals** - Payroll departments aren't villains. Systems are complex.

5. **DO focus on empowerment** - The user is taking control. That's the story.
