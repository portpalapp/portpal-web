# PORTPAL 90-Day Social Media Content Calendar

## Overview

**Campaign Period:** Week 1 - Week 13 (90 Days)
**Primary Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram
**Posting Frequency:** 1-2 posts per day during launch, 1 post per day ongoing
**Target Audience:** BC Longshoremen (ILWU Local 500, 502)

---

## Optimal Posting Times for Blue-Collar Workers

| Time Slot | Best For | Why |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| **5:30-6:30 AM** | Morning dispatch crowd | Workers checking phones before heading to dispatch hall |
| **11:30 AM-12:30 PM** | Lunch break | Mid-shift phone check |
| **6:00-8:00 PM** | After shift | Unwinding at home, highest engagement window |
| **Saturday/Sunday AM** | Weekend workers | Catching up on content before weekend shifts |

**Primary posting windows:** 6:00 AM and 6:30 PM daily

## Production Guidelines

### Video Length by Platform
- **TikTok:** 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 30-45 seconds)
- **Instagram Reels:** 15-60 seconds
- **Instagram Stories:** 15 seconds max

### Visual Style
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- Blue primary color (matches app)
- High contrast text overlays
- Port/terminal b-roll when possible
- NO lifestyle flex content
- NO salary/earnings focus

### Captions
- Always include (accessibility)
- White text with black outline
- Bottom third of screen

### Music/Sound
- Trending TikTok sounds when appropriate
- Original voiceover for educational content
- Avoid copyrighted music for IG
