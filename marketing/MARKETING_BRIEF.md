PORTPAL helps port workers (longshoremen) track their shifts, verify pay, and catch payroll errors. Think of it as a "pay stub detective" for union workers with complex pay structures.

- $41M in pay tracked

## Target Customer: The Casual Worker

Casual workers (part-time/on-call) are our BEST customers because:

1. **Irregular schedules** - Work different jobs at different terminals daily
2. **Complex pay** - Hard to track when every day is different
3. **Seniority tracking** - Need to log hours to advance their career
4. **More errors** - Dispatched to various jobs = more pay mistakes

Casuals are highly motivated to verify their pay and track their hours.
