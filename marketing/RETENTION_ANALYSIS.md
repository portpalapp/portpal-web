# PORTPAL Retention Analysis Report

**Analysis Date:** February 1, 2026
**Data Period:** August 2023 - February 2026
**Total Users Analyzed:** 752
**Total Shifts Logged:** 71,706

---

## Executive Summary

PORTPAL shows strong engagement among core users but faces significant early-stage churn challenges. Key findings:

- **57.4% of users remain active** (shift in last 30 days)
- **42.6% churn rate** overall
- **9.0% of users log only 1 shift and never return**
- **Power users (5+ shifts/month) have dramatically lower churn (27.8%)** vs non-power users (84.6%)
- **Month 1 retention averages 62.8%** - the first month is critical
- **Users who log 10+ shifts in their first month have 69.9% retention**

## Appendix: Methodology

### Definitions

- **Active User:** Has logged at least 1 shift in the last 30 days
- **Churned User:** No shift logged in 30+ days
- **Power User:** Averages 5+ shifts per month
- **Cohort:** Users grouped by their first shift month
- **At-Risk:** 14-30 days since last shift (not yet churned, but trending toward it)

### Data Quality Notes

- Total records: 71,712
- Valid records after cleaning: 71,706
- Unique users: 752
- Date range: August 2023 - February 2026

### Analysis Assumptions

- Reference date for churn calculation: February 1, 2026
- First shift date = user "signup" date for cohort analysis
- Retention calculated as % of cohort still active at each month milestone
