PORTPAL Social Media Guide

Instagram & TikTok Content Playbook

What is PORTPAL?

An app that helps longshoremen (port workers) track their shifts and make sure they're getting paid correctly.

One-liner: "The pay stub detective for port workers"

Who We're Talking To

  • • Longshoremen - dock workers at ports (Vancouver, BC area)
  • • Casuals - part-time workers who need to track hours for seniority
  • • Age range: 25-55, mostly male
  • • Vibe: Blue-collar, no-BS, union proud, hard workers

Key Messages (Use These!)

"Know your worth before payday"

"Track your hours, protect your pay"

"Stop guessing what your paycheck should be"

"Built by longshoremen, for longshoremen"

"Your pay stub detective"

Content Pillars (5 Types of Posts)

1. PAY WINS 💰

Stories of catching pay errors, getting money back

Examples: "User found $847 missing from their pay" / "That feeling when PORTPAL catches an error"

2. DOCK LIFE 🚢

Relatable content about working at the port

Examples: Early morning dispatch / Crew camaraderie / Weather challenges / "You know you're a longshoreman when..."

3. PAY EDUCATION 📚

Tips about understanding pay, OT, differentials

Examples: "Did you know night shift pays 26% more?" / "How graveyard OT is calculated"

4. APP TIPS 📱

Quick tutorials, feature highlights

Examples: "How to log a shift in 10 seconds" / "New feature: pay predictions"

5. UNION PRIDE 💪

Celebrating longshoremen, union strength

Examples: ILWU history / Longshore legends / "Respect the hustle"

Tone & Style

DO ✓

  • • Be direct, no fluff
  • • Use worker language
  • • Show real numbers
  • • Respect the hustle
  • • Keep it simple
  • • Be relatable

DON'T ✗

  • • Corporate speak
  • • Overly polished
  • • Condescending
  • • Too salesy
  • • Ignore comments
  • • Use stock photos

Visual Style

Primary: Emerald green (#10b981)
Background: Dark slate (#1e293b)
Accent: Blue (#3b82f6)

Fonts: Bold, clean, easy to read. No fancy scripts.
Images: Real dock/port imagery, workers, ships, containers. Gritty > polished.

Quick Reference

App name: PORTPAL (all caps)

What it does: Track shifts, verify pay, catch errors

Price: Free to try, $99/year for Pro
