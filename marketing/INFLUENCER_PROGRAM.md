## Program Overview

PORTPAL is the shift-tracking and pay management app built specifically for BC port workers. We partner with authentic voices in the longshore, trucking, and skilled trades communities to share how PORTPAL simplifies the complexity of tracking variable pay rates, shift differentials, and overtime.

**Our Mission:** Empower blue-collar workers to understand and verify their pay with confidence.

**Why Partner with PORTPAL:**
- Be part of a product built BY port workers, FOR port workers
- Authentic brand that values worker advocacy
- Competitive compensation with performance bonuses
- Early access to features and direct input on product development
- Join a growing community of industry creators

## Ideal Creator Profile

### Primary Industries

**1. Longshoremen / Port Workers (Highest Priority)**
- Active or retired dock workers
- ILWU members or adjacent unions
- Port operations content creators
- Marine industry professionals

**2. Trucking & Transportation**
- Port truckers and drayage operators
- Long-haul truckers with hourly pay
- Logistics and supply chain creators
- Fleet operators and owner-operators

**3. Skilled Trades**
- Mechanics (especially heavy-duty)
- Welders, electricians, millwrights
- Construction workers with variable pay
- Shift workers in industrial settings

**4. Blue-Collar Lifestyle**
- Work-life balance content
- Financial literacy for trades
- Career advice for labor industries
- Union and worker advocacy

### Content Style Preferences

**What We Love:**
- Authentic, unpolished content (real work environments)
- Educational content explaining industry complexity
- Day-in-the-life vlogs
- Humor and relatability
- Problem/solution storytelling
- Community-focused content

**What We Avoid:**
- Overly polished, disconnected production
- Controversial political content
- Negative industry commentary
- Unsafe work practice demonstrations
- Content that could embarrass employers or unions

### Audience Demographics

**Target Audience Alignment:**
| Metric | Ideal Range |
|--------|-------------|
| Age | 25-55 years old |
| Gender | Any (industry skews 85% male) |
| Location | BC, Canada (primary); Pacific Northwest, USA (secondary) |
| Income | $60,000 - $150,000 household |
| Interests | Work, family, financial security, vehicles, sports |

**Platform Preferences:**
- TikTok: Younger demographic, viral potential
- Instagram: Balanced demographic, strong engagement
- YouTube: Long-form education, highest conversion
- Facebook: Older demographic, community groups

### Engagement Rate Expectations

| Platform | Nano | Micro | Mid | Macro |
|----------|------|-------|-----|-------|
| TikTok | 8%+ | 6%+ | 4%+ | 3%+ |
| Instagram Reels | 5%+ | 4%+ | 3%+ | 2%+ |
| Instagram Feed | 4%+ | 3%+ | 2.5%+ | 2%+ |
| YouTube | 5%+ | 4%+ | 3%+ | 2%+ |

**Red Flags:**
- Engagement rate below 1.5% (potential fake followers)
- High follower count with low view counts
- Comment sections with generic spam
- Sudden follower spikes without viral content
- No content related to work or industry

## Content Ideas for Creators

**Concept:** Naturally integrate PORTPAL into existing day-in-the-life content.

**Script Framework:**
```
[Morning routine / driving to work]
"Getting ready for another day shift at [terminal].
Let me log this shift real quick..."

[Show app - logging shift]
"Day shift at [location], working [job].
PORTPAL already knows my rate is [rate] with the [differential]."

[Later in video]
"Shift's done. That's [hours] at [rate].
PORTPAL says I should be getting [amount] for today.
We'll see if the pay stub matches."

[End of video]
"Link to try PORTPAL in my bio."
```

**Best Practices:**
- Keep PORTPAL integration to 20-30 seconds in longer content
- Show the actual app screen
- Make it feel like a natural part of your routine
