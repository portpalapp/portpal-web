# PORTPAL Social Media Marketing Plan

## The Problem We're Solving (Emotional Core)

**Pain Points (ranked by emotional intensity):**

1. **"Am I getting robbed?"** - Pay discrepancies happen constantly. $34 here, $50 there. Over a career? Thousands.
2. **"Will I make pension this year?"** - The anxiety of not knowing if you'll hit $120k
3. **"I'm leaving money on the table"** - Not knowing which jobs/shifts actually pay more
4. **"30 years and still using paper"** - Embarrassingly outdated tracking methods
5. **"The company never makes mistakes in YOUR favor"** - Systemic distrust

**Target Audience:**
- BC Longshoremen (ILWU Local 500, 502, etc.)
- Age 25-55, mostly male
- Union proud, working class
- Tech-skeptical but smartphone-dependent
- Value: family, financial security, fairness, solidarity

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. RAGE/JUSTICE (40% of content)
*Hook emotion: Anger, validation*
- Pay discrepancies exposed
- "They're hoping you don't notice"
- Collective power messaging

### 2. MONEY/OPTIMIZATION (30% of content)
*Hook emotion: Greed, FOMO*
- "This shift pays $200 more than that one"
- Pension goal strategies
- Highest paying job breakdowns

### 3. TRANSFORMATION (20% of content)
*Hook emotion: Aspiration, relief*
- Before/after tracking
- "I used to stress about pension..."
- Time saved, peace of mind

### 4. EDUCATION/VALUE (10% of content)
*Hook emotion: Empowerment*
- Rate breakdowns
- Collective agreement explainers
- How differentials work

## TikTok Content Strategy

### Format Mix:
- 60% Talking head with text overlays
- 20% Screen recordings of app
- 15% Carousels (educational)
- 5% Memes/trending sounds

### Posting Schedule:
- 2x daily during launch week
- 1x daily ongoing
- Best times: 6-7am (before dispatch), 6-8pm (after shift)
