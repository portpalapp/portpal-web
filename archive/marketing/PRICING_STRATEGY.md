# PORTPAL Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Tiers

### FREE TIER - "Casual"
*The basics to get the job done. For our brothers and sisters.*

**Included:**
- Shift logging (unlimited)
- Automatic pay rate calculation
- Weekly shift summary (view & save)
- Basic earnings dashboard (this week, last week)
- Pension year progress bar
- 1 AI question per week (random prompt)
- Job prediction: Once weekly (random day)

**Not Included:**
- Callback (repeat yesterday) feature
- Custom templates
- Unlimited AI chat
- Pay stub upload & reconciliation
- Daily job predictions
- Advanced analytics
- Pay discrepancy alerts
- Data export

---

### PRO TIER - $99/year or $10/month
*Full power. Every feature. Peace of mind.*

**Everything in Free, PLUS:**

**Time Savers:**
- Callback feature (repeat yesterday with 1 tap)
- Custom templates (save your common shifts)
- Quick log from notification

**AI Power:**
- Unlimited AI questions
- "What if" scenario planning
- Pension goal optimization
- Time-off impact calculator
- Personalized job recommendations

**Pay Protection:**
- Pay stub upload (photo)
- AI-powered reconciliation
- Discrepancy alerts
- Detailed pay breakdown analysis
- Historical rate comparison

**Analytics Pro:**
- Daily job predictions (based on seniority + patterns)
- Earnings trends over time
- Job/location/shift breakdowns
- Year-over-year comparison
- Custom date range reports

**Extras:**
- Data export (CSV, PDF)
- Unlimited history
- Priority support
- Early access to new features

## Launch Pricing

### Founding Member Offer (First 500 Users)
- **$79/year** (20% off forever)
- Or **$8/month**
- Badge: "Founding Member" in app
- Locked-in rate for life

### Standard Pricing (After launch)
- **$99/year** (save $21 vs monthly)

- No credit card required to start
- Prompt to upgrade at day 7 and 13
- Graceful downgrade to Free after trial

## Revenue Projections

**Assumptions:**
- BC has ~3,000 active longshoremen
- Realistic market capture: 20% = 600 users
- Conversion to Pro: 40% = 240 paying users

**Conservative (240 Pro users):**
- Annual: $23,760/year
- Monthly: $1,980/month

**Optimistic (600 Pro users):**
- Annual: $59,400/year
- Monthly: $4,950/month

**Break-even:**
- If hosting/API costs ~$200/month
- Need ~25 Pro users to cover costs

## Cancellation Flow

1. Ask why (survey: too expensive, not using, missing feature, other)
2. Offer pause (1-3 months) instead of cancel
3. Offer downgrade to free (keep data)
4. If "too expensive" - offer 50% off for 3 months
5. Confirm cancellation, data retained for 1 year
